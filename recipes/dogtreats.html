Dog Treats

Delicious baked dog treats for your pup

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 800 grams of lamb liver
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Cut lamb liver into pieces
  2. Whisk 4 eggs
  3. Combine lamb liver, eggs and flour into the blender
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Pour onto a silicon sheet and bake for 20 minutes at 180 degrees celcius
