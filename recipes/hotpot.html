Chinese Hot Pot

Sharing is caring, yummy hot pot for the family

Ingredients

  • Sliced pork and beef
  • Pork balls
  • Tofu
  • Mushrooms
  • Chinese cabbage sliced
  • Soup stock

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Boil the soup stock in a large pot
  3. Prepare the dipping sauce
  4. Cook the ingredients as required for eating
